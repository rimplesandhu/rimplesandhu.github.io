1. **Biological Computing as Energy-efficient Alternative to Digital Computing** (2025–present)
   *Funding:* LDRD, U.S. Department of Energy
   Exploring biochemical and biological computing paradigms as low-power alternatives to digital computing using control theory and active inference frameworks.

1. **Multisensor Data Fusion of Traffic Trajectory Data at Edge** (2021–present)
   *Funding:* LDRD, U.S. DOE; SMART Program, U.S. DOT
   Developing edge-computing pipelines for fusing data from roadside lidar, radar, and camera sensors using Kalman filtering and multi-sensor fusion methods.

1. **Stochastic Movement Modeling of Soaring Raptors using Telemetry Data** (2020–2023)
   *Funding:* Wind Energy Technologies Office, U.S. DOE
   Stochastic agent-based modeling of soaring raptor flight near wind energy infrastructure using GPS telemetry data and atmospheric updraft simulations.

1. **Physics-informed Bayesian Learning of Nonlinear Dynamical Systems** (2014–2019)
   *Funding:* NSERC Canada; Ontario Graduate Scholarship; Carleton University
   Bayesian model selection, sparse learning, and parameter estimation for nonlinear physics-based aeroelastic models validated against wind-tunnel experiments.

1. **Surrogate-powered Sensitivity Analysis for High-fidelity Aeroelastic Simulations** (2018–2019)
   *Funding:* National Research Council Canada
   Global sensitivity analysis of transonic flutter using surrogate models coupled with high-fidelity CFD-CSD solvers.
